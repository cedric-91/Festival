RUNWAY FESTIVAL 2017

We are delighted to invite you to the first ever Runway Festival to be held at Perranproth Airfield on Saturday 26th and Sunday 27th August from 10am until 5pm, gates close at 6pm. This event promises to be an exciting mix of adrenaline, action and adventure both on and off the runway. The festival will include over 10 motor exhibitors, including Damerell's Motorcycles, Carrs Landrover and Jaguar, Dales Motor Group, Telsa and more.

ON THE TRACK

From adrenaline-fuelled desmonstrations to watching the runway behind the wheel, here's what you can expect on the board:

  • Try before you buy - no pressure, cruise the cars in your own time and if you to make a purchase, make the most of the exclusive event deals
  • Ride the runway - visitors will be able to road test cars from more than ten manufactures and car dealers
  • Learn to drive - 14 or over? Feel what it's like to push down on the pedals in a safe but stimulating environment
  • Strengthen your skills - test your skid skills in our designated skid car
  • Learn from the best - be in the know as motor club guest speakers discuss the lastest motoring news

OFF THE TRACK

It's not just about the mighty motors, off the runway there is an exciting line-up of events that will entertain the whole family.

  • Have fun at the fairground - it's rides galore as we welcome the classic carousel and whizzing waltzer
  • Battle of Bands - discover great Cornish talent as they play music all weekend
  • Food, glorious food - scrumptious street food and local produce at our food quarters
  • The great Cornish Cake Off - think you're a dab hand at baking? We go live with local chefs to find our Cornish cake champion
  • Soap box college challenge
  • Static aircraft display

SKY HIGH

If speed on the ground wasn't enough, look up to the sky for even more adrenaline action:

  • Pleasure flights around the air base
  • Learn to fly with Perranporth flying club
  • There will be parachuting from the Cornwall parachute club
  • Take an exhilarating micro-light trip
  • Learn to parachute all weekend with a tandem sky dive
